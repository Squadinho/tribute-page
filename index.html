Hodor's life

  • A Game of Thrones: In service of House Stark as a stableboy. Following Bran Stark's fall, Hodor carries the boy round in a basket on his back.
  • A Clash of Kings: Survives the sacking of Winterfell by hiding in the crypts below the city. Eventually escapes Winterfell to travel north to the Wall with Bran, Jojen and Meera.
  • A Storm of Swords: With Bran, Jojen and Meera, ventures to the North side of the Wall, meeting Coldhands.
  • A Dance with Dragons: Fights off Wights outside the cave of the three-eyed crow, (after skinchanger Bran takes control of Hodor).
